Why would you want your own Lantern?

I'm a Funder
Tell me more

Your Open Access policy is an important part of your identity, and you want researchers, institutions and publishers you fund to comply with it. You may want to take action if you find your policy isn't being met, and to do that you need to quickly and easily identify publications which haven't quite lived up to your standards. Finding those publications in your returns from institutions can be time consuming and costly, and Lantern makes that process much easier.

With your own Lantern you can make it easy for your compliance team to review your returns from institutions, gathering exactly the information you are interested in, and even having compliance to your policy automatically calculated for you. Not only that, you can ask institutions and researchers to use your branded interface themselves, to ensure that they are compliant before they submit their returns.

Contact us now if this sounds interesting.

I'm a Research Institution
Tell me more

As one of a growing number of institutions developing and promoting their Open Access policies, it's important for you to know how adoption and compliance is progressing. You may also have requirements placed upon you by external parties, such as government or funders, and you want to be sure you can meet their policies too. You can use Lantern for checking your institutional publication records as well as each of your funder or REF returns. If you identify publcations which do not live up to those standards, you can work with your researchers to improve access to their work.

With your own Lantern you can brand and embed the system in your institutional website, making it easy for researchers to check their publications within the context of their home institution. Your Open Access team can also check your publications in bulk and gather exactly the information they are interested in, and even have compliance to your your own Open Access policy calculated for you. With access to our API, you can also integrate compliance lookups into your repository, research information system, or research management system.

Contact us now if this sounds interesting.

I'm a Publisher
Tell me more

As a publisher adapting to the way Open Access has changed the landscape, it's important for you to understand how you are perceived by the research community. They are working to meet institutional, funder and government policies on the availability of their work, and you play a big part in that. Whether you publish Open Access, or a mixture of Open and traditional articles, you may wish to use Lantern to check your own list of publications to determine how they appear to your users and those they report to.

With your own Lantern, using your branding, you can make a strong statement to the community that you are committed to Open Access. Meanwhile, your team can check your publication back catalogue, and monitor ongoing publications, gathering exactly the information they need to act on. This might result in changes to how you present content on your website, or lead to conversations with third parties on how they represent your material to maximise your presence.

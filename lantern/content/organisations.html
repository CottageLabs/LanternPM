Information for Organisations

For an annual subscription, your organisation can have its own customised, branded version of Lantern, hosted and maintained for you by our team. You get:

Dedicated Support

You'll receive an email address for a dedicated support service.

Your organisation's branding

Use of your logo and colour scheme, and available at your own domain name.

Customised compliance calculations

Tell us about your policy or mandate, and we can include this in your output.

Customised output fields.

Select which of the large range of Lantern fields you want in your output.

Manage your own users

Full user and jobs management dashboard.

Access to our API

Integrate your local systems directly with your Lantern instance.

If you want to hear more from an organisation that uses this service, take a look at our Wellcome Trust Case Study.

Why would you want your own Lantern?

  • Funder - ensure that the organisations you fund can meet your policy or mandate before they submit their returns to you.
  • Institution - ensure that your researchers can meet your policy or mandate, and manage your ongoing open access agenda
  • Publisher - ensure that your open access publications appear to the world the way you want them to appear

How to get your own Lantern

  1. Just contact us. We'll get back to you as quickly as we can.
  2. We'll discuss your requirements with you: your branding, your configuration, and your domain name
  3. We'll create your Lantern instance in our testing hub, and you can play with it and tweak it until you're happy
  4. We'll work with your IT department to configure your domain name
  5. We'll help you out with tutorials, and be available for your support questions
